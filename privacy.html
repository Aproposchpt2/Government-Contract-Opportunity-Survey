Survey Privacy

Your Participation Should Be Clear, Voluntary, and Respected

This notice explains what the survey collects and how responses may be used.

Purpose of the Survey

The Government Contract Opportunity Survey is independent market research conducted by Apropos Group LLC. Its purpose is to better understand the challenges businesses and support organizations face when discovering, evaluating, and pursuing government contract opportunities.

Information Collected

The survey collects the answers you choose to provide, including your respondent category, government markets of interest, contracting experience, common challenges, current search methods, and interest in opportunity-evaluation or proposal-development support.

The survey may also record the campaign source used to reach the survey, such as LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, email, a partner organization, or a QR code. This helps evaluate which outreach channels reach relevant participants.

Anonymous Participation

You may complete the survey without providing your name, business name, email address, telephone number, or website. Contact information is optional.

Optional Contact Information

When you voluntarily provide contact information and authorize follow-up, Apropos Group LLC may contact you only regarding the research topics or follow-up interests you selected.

How Responses May Be Used

Survey responses may be analyzed in aggregate for market research, service planning, business-development analysis, and improvement of resources intended to help businesses participate more effectively in the government marketplace.

Individual responses will not be publicly attributed to you without permission.

Information This Survey Does Not Request

Do not submit government passwords, SAM.gov credentials, banking information, tax records, Social Security numbers, Employer Identification Numbers, proprietary solicitation documents, confidential proposal files, or other sensitive credentials through this survey.

Data Processing

Survey submissions are processed through the hosting provider's form-submission system. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards should be maintained for access to submitted responses. No internet-based submission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Voluntary Participation

Participation is voluntary. You may leave the survey before submitting it, skip the optional written-response and contact sections, or submit the survey anonymously.

Questions

Questions regarding this survey or its privacy practices may be directed to Apropos Group LLC through the contact information associated with the survey campaign.
